Travel itinerary suggestion

Based on the information provided by the user, we will provide the most reasonable route to the user, with time and cost optimization according to the user's requirements. All you need to do is answer simple questions and provide accurate information about the itinerary you want to take.

Favorite

Users can favorite routes as well as locations that they are interested in. With these favorites, the users can create their own library to store suitable locations and routes for themselves. Furthermore, users have the option to make their created routes public so that others can use them as a reference.

Edit travel itinerary

With the desire to provide the best experiences to our users, we always bring them valuable experiences. If users want to change their route, they can do so immediately when our system provides them with itinerary suggestions based on the information they have provided.
